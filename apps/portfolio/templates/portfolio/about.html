About Luke

Hi, my name is Luke and I am an artist, but not in the way you might think. I am what most would consider a "Jack-of-all-trades". The reason being, I love to create!! One thing about my loving to be creative is that I take that passion into all areas of my life. Whether it's singing, being funny, cooking, songwriting, or writing code.. I love that sensation of taking an idea from start to finish; from empty pots and pans to beautiful desserts for people to enjoy, from a blank sheet of paper to a fully composed song, or from a blank screen to complete deployment (with updates here and there).

I graduated from the University of Valley Forge in 2014 with a Bachelors degree in Music Performance, and now I'm learning how to develop web applications through CodingDojo. I find that I love learning, and it is a part of who I am as an artist. As an artist, I've always had a very simple instinct to want to make things stand out, and really "breathe life" back into things. As a developer, I've had to learn how to balance that instinct with functionality and usability. It has kept things interesting for me and I'm happy to say that I'm fully engaged in the pursuit of mastery in this field.

When I'm not working on projects for clients/employers, you'll find me either playing music, giving music lesson, or being goofy!! I love learning and teaching, and if I can look back and say that I'm better today than I was yesterday, then that makes all the efforts of today worth it. Anyway.. Thanks for visiting my portfolio! P.S. Email me to take a peek at my resume!
